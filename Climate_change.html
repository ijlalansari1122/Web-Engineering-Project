Understanding Climate Change

Explore the impact of climate change on Gilgit-Baltistan and how we can address it.

What is Climate Change?

Climate change refers to significant changes in global temperatures and weather patterns over time. Human activities, such as burning fossil fuels and deforestation, contribute to the accumulation of greenhouse gases in the atmosphere, leading to global warming and adverse effects on the environment.

Impact on Gilgit-Baltistan

Gilgit-Baltistan, with its unique geographical features and climate, is particularly vulnerable to the effects of climate change. Rising temperatures, changing precipitation patterns, and increased frequency of extreme weather events are impacting local ecosystems, agriculture, and water resources.

Our Actions

We are committed to raising awareness about climate change and advocating for sustainable practices. Our initiatives include community education, promoting renewable energy, and supporting policies that mitigate climate change impacts.

How You Can Help

Everyone can contribute to combating climate change. Simple actions such as reducing energy consumption, using public transportation, and supporting local environmental organizations can make a difference. Join us in our efforts to protect our environment and ensure a sustainable future for Gilgit-Baltistan.

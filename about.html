About GB Climate Initiative

Dedicated to preserving the natural beauty and resilience of Gilgit-Baltistan.

Our Mission

At GB Climate Initiative, our mission is to raise awareness and take actionable steps towards combating climate change in the Gilgit-Baltistan region. We focus on education, community engagement, and sustainable practices to ensure a safe and vibrant future for all.

What We Do

We organize workshops, seminars, and community events to educate the public on the impacts of climate change and the importance of disaster preparedness. Our efforts also include collaborating with local authorities on implementing effective climate action plans.

Our Vision

We envision a Gilgit-Baltistan where communities are resilient, the environment is protected, and future generations can thrive in harmony with nature. By working together, we can make this vision a reality.
